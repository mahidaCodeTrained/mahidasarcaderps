The rules for Mahida Arcade's Rock Paper Scissors!

1. Rock always beats Scissors.

2. Paper always beats Rock.

3. Scissors always beats Paper.

4. If both you the player and the computer select the same option then it's a draw no points are awarded.

5. The last rule is to have fun and try to beat the computer over to you!
